ABSTRACT

Image manipulation and forgery has been a severe cyber threat, ever since the inception of social media. It potentially conveys a false news or claim with an underlying malicious intent such as invoking communal violence. Here a deep learning algorithm is being used to identify 385 different kinds of image forgery using a novel neural network architecture called Manipulation Tracing Network (ManTraNet).

Based upon the original 2019 paper by Wu et. al.
